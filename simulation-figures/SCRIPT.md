# The Center — narrative beat sheet

A companion to the *From Challenge to Outcome* vision piece. Where that piece sells the
**methodology** (one problem flowing through the pipeline), this one sells the **physical
expression** of it: the Center as zoned space — *"not a building, a capability you walk
through."*

The thesis, made visual: the six zones sit on a glowing **substrate** labelled
**Methodology · Governance · Technology** — the innovation operating system they run on.

## Layout — 3 clusters, 6 zones

A top-down/isometric floor plan. Three clusters as columns (left → right), two zones each.

## Beats (~50s, then free-explore)

0. **Intro (wide).** Establishing shot of the whole floor plan on its substrate.
   *"An innovation center isn't a building — it's a capability you can walk through."*
1. **Zone 1 — Mission Framing & Collaboration** *(Think Tank).* Where the challenge is defined.
2. **Zone 6 — Knowledge & Research Hub** *(Think Tank).* Every challenge leaves reusable knowledge.
3. **Zone 2 — Digital Experimentation Lab** *(Workshop Floor).* Prove it in software first.
4. **Zone 3 — Rapid Prototyping & Integration Lab** *(Workshop Floor).* Where ideas become things.
5. **Zone 4 — Test & Evaluation Area** *(Showcase).* Controlled proof before the field.
6. **Zone 5 — Showcase & Demonstration Area** *(Showcase).* Where the center tells its story.
7. **Finale (wide).** Substrate brightens; *"Six zones. One capability."*

Each zone beat lifts and lights its tile, opens the right-side inspector (purpose + kit),
and advances the progress pips. At the end the camera hands off to orbit; click any tile to
inspect it.

## How it maps to the pitch
- **One scene, three deliverables:** the same floor plan is the ~50s tour video (one camera
  path), the still frames (pause anywhere), and the interactive blueprint (drive it yourself).
- **Defining what the architecture must support — not the architecture.** Each zone is named
  by capability, not by furniture.
